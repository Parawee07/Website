Prep time

5 mins

Cook time

30 mins

2 Servings

1 mins

Tags: Quick Recipe

Instructions

Step 1

In a saucepan, heat the butter over medium heat until it turns brown. This will take about 3-5minutes. Be careful not to burn it. Once brownede, remove it from the heat.

Step 2

Add the coconut (or granulated) sugar and brown sugar to the browned butter. Whisk the mixture until it resembles wet sand

Step 3

Stir in the egg and splash of vanilla extract into the butter-sugar mixture. Mix well until the egg is fully incorparated.

Step 4

Sift the all-purpose flour and baking powder into the wet mixture using a strainer. Gently mix with a spatula until the flour is fully combined. The dough should be soft and slightly sticky.

Step 5

Add the cocoa nibs (or chocolate chips) into the dough and mix until they are evenly distributed.

Step 6

If the dough feels too dry, add 1 tablespoon of milk and knead the dough with your hands to incorporate it evenly.

Step 7

Shape the dough into a ball and wrap it with plastic wrap. Refrigerate the dough for at least 40 minutes. Chilling the dough will prevent the cookies from spreading too much during baking.

Step 8

Preheat your oven to 175 degrees Celsius (350 degrees Fahrenheit) for about 10 minutes.

Step 9

Line a baking sheet with parchment paper or a silicone baking mat.

Step 10

Take the chilled dough out of the fridge and form small balls (about 1-2 tablespoons) of dough. Place them on the prepared baking sheet, leaving enough space between each cookie to allow for spreading.

Step 11

Gently flatten each ball with the palm of your hand or the back of a spoon.

Step 12

Sprinkle a pinch of sea salt over each cookie for a delightful salty-sweet contrast.

Step 13

Bake the cookies in the preheated oven for approximately 12 minutes or until the edges turn golden brown.

Step 14

Once baked, remove the cookies from the oven and let them cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.

Ingredients

  • 115g butter
  • 70g coconut (or granulated) sugar
  • 120g brown sugar
  • 1 egg
  • vanilla essence
  • 175g all purpose flour
  • 3g baking soda
  • 1tbsp milk
  • cocoa nibs (or chocolate chips)
  • sea salt
